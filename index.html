FAQ

Questions? We've Got
Clear Answers

Everything small business owners usually ask before getting started.

Do I need to pay first before seeing the design? ▼
No! We always show you the design preview first. You only pay after you've seen it and confirmed you're happy. This is our trust-based process — zero upfront risk for you.
How long does it take to build my website? ▼
Most websites are delivered within 3–5 working days after payment. Pro package clients get priority delivery in 2–3 days. We're fast because we've built many sites and know what works.
What do I need to provide to get started? ▼
Just your business name, the type of website you need, your contact number, and any photos or text you want on the site. If you don't have photos, we can use high-quality free stock images.
Will my website work on mobile phones? ▼
Yes, 100% guaranteed. Every website we build is fully mobile-responsive. Since most of your customers will find you on their phones, this is a top priority for us.
Can I request changes after seeing the preview? ▼
Absolutely. The preview stage is exactly for that. We'll revise the design based on your feedback before you pay. Once you're happy, then we proceed with payment and final delivery.
Do you offer hosting? Where will the website be hosted? ▼
We can help you set up affordable hosting — there are options starting from ₹100–200/month. We'll guide you through the entire process. Just ask us on WhatsApp and we'll recommend the best option for your budget.
